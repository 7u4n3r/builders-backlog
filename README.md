# Builders Backlog

A structured backlog system for unfinished ideas, fragmented research, half-built tools, and project context.

Builders Backlog is built for people who are carrying too many ideas across too many places: notes apps, screenshots, tabs, folders, drafts, and half-finished builds.

It gives unfinished work somewhere to land before it becomes a product, post, tool, system, or decision.

## What It Helps With

- Capture unfinished ideas before they disappear
- Hold research and build context in one place
- Track half-built tools, pages, products, and projects
- Sort scattered thoughts into usable next steps
- Preserve momentum without forcing everything into a finished plan too early

## Who It Is For

- Builders
- Researchers
- Creators
- Indie devs
- AI tinkerers
- People with too many tabs open
- People whose best ideas keep getting buried

## Why It Exists

Most tools are built for clean projects.

Builders Backlog is built for the messy middle.

The part where the idea is real, but not ready yet.

## Simple Promise

Stop losing usable ideas just because they are not finished yet.
